I missed this while I was on vacation, but Read It Later re-branded itself as Pocket. Overall, it’s a beautiful update. It’s another app to point to when people say that you can’t make pretty applications on Android.

I have minor concerns with them making it into a free app. I don’t think they’ll switch to ads, but I still imagine the worst until they actually tell us their plan.
