Physics is awesome. They can completely calculate everything about the Mars descent ahead of time, and tell a computer to do it all.

And they have to, because Mars is currently 7 light minutes away from Earth. That’d be like playing Tie Fighter with a ping of 840000ms, or 14 minutes of latency.
