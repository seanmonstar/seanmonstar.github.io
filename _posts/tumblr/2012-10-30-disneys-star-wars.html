You’ve likely already heard: Disney bought Lucasfilm. And they plan to release a new Star Wars movie in 2015.

If they go the route of KOTOR, and create a brand new story like 1000 years before the trilogy, this could be pretty cool. If they continue Luke’s story after Return of the Jedi, and trample over the canonical Expanded Universe, I think Star Wars fans will burn down Disneyland.
