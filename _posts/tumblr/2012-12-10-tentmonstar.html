I’ve been pretty pumped about Tent, so much so that I’ve written an Android client1. I actually put it in the Google Play Store 2 weeks ago, but forgot to link to it.

  • View your feed of statuses and reposts
  • View profiles
  • Follow/unfollow others
  • Create new statuses and reposts
  • Highlight posts that mention you
  • Share to tentmonstar

Get it in the Play Store, and let me know if there are issues.

  1. The client will do you no good without a Tent server. You can set your own up, or register at tent.is. ↩
